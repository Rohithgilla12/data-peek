# 8-Week dev.to Series

Publish one article per week. Repurpose each post for one primary and one secondary subreddit with different framing.

## Article Structure Template (Use Every Week)

1. 2-3 sentence founder or user problem intro.
2. Concrete implementation section with examples.
3. 3 lessons learned (1 success, 1 failure, 1 open question).
4. Short conclusion with one specific feedback request.

## Repurposing Rules

- Do not paste dev.to article directly to Reddit.
- Keep Reddit body self-contained and useful without any link click.
- Change title, opening paragraph, and final question per subreddit.
- Include one visual (screenshot or short clip) where subreddit norms favor demos.
